## 2. BRAND VOICE/TONE RECOMMENDATIONS

### The Tension: Institutionalised vs. Approachable

Evolution Stables faces a classic brand paradox:
- **Need:** Credibility with institutional investors (regulated, professional)
- **Need:** Accessibility to attract new retail participants

### Recommendation: "Accessible Institutional"

| Dimension | Pure Institutional | Current Evolution | Recommended |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Tone** | Stiff, academic | Professional, confident | Professional but conversational |
| **Vocabulary** | Technical jargon | Technical but accessible | Same |
| **Emotion** | None allowed | Measured pride | Quiet confidence + occasional warmth |
| **CTAs** | "Contact our team" | "Join the Evolution" | Keep "Join the Evolution" |
| **Visuals** | Cold, sterile | Dark/gold/professional | Add subtle motion/emotion |

### Voice Guidelines by Channel

**Instagram/TikTok Reels:**
- Keep institutional visual aesthetic (dark/gold)
- Use confident, declarative statements
- Allow slightly more energy than website copy
- Never sacrifice legitimacy for entertainment

**Website/Blog:**
- Most formal expression of the brand
- Lead with FMA compliance and regulatory proof
- Use "professionalise" not "simplify"

**Email/CRM:**
- Personalized but professional
- "Welcome to the Evolution" (warm institutional)

## 4. CONTENT TEMPLATES

### Template A: "Weekend Recap" (Performance/Hype)
**Purpose:** Showcase race wins, build momentum
**Tone:** Confident, data-forward, quietly proud
**Structure:**
```
00:00-00:03 | Hook: "First rule of ownership... wrong numbers"
00:03-00:07 | Title: "WEEKEND RECAP [DATE]"
00:07-00:25 | Race 1: Location, horse name, result, prizemoney
00:25-00:43 | Race 2: Location, horse name, result, prizemoney
00:43-00:58 | Race 3+: Continue pattern
00:58-01:05 | CTA: "Join the Evolution" + next race date
```
**Visual Treatment:** Fast cuts (2-3s), gold text overlays, Ken Burns on stills
**Audio:** Tension bed (Social Network style), duck under voiceover

### Template B: "Horse Profile" (Story/Investment)
**Purpose:** Introduce individual horses, build emotional + rational case
**Tone:** Analytical storytelling, heritage meets data
**Structure:**
```
00:00-00:03 | Hook: Horse name in gold, pedigree line
00:03-00:12 | Heritage: Sire/dam, breeding value
00:12-00:25 | Performance: Race record, earnings, growth curve
00:25-00:35 | Ownership: Syndicate structure, price point
00:35-00:45 | Vision: Trainer quote, future races
00:45-00:50 | CTA: "Own the experience" + waitlist link
```
**Visual Treatment:** Slower cuts (4-5s), mixed stills and footage, pedigree charts
**Audio:** Minimal piano, atmospheric, voiceover-led

### Template C: "Institutional Proof" (Trust/Authority)
**Purpose:** Establish credibility, FMA compliance, regulatory positioning
**Tone:** Measured, factual, confident
**Structure:**
```
00:00-00:05 | Hook: "This isn't betting. This is ownership."
00:05-00:15 | Problem: Traditional ownership barriers (cost, complexity)
00:15-00:30 | Solution: Tokenized ownership, FMA framework
00:30-00:40 | Proof: Dubai Racing Club partnership, real assets
00:40-00:50 | Vision: "Professionalise your racehorse ownership"
00:50-00:55 | CTA: Email capture / waitlist
```
**Visual Treatment:** Clean graphics, data visualizations, institutional imagery
**Audio:** Documentary-style, understated, authoritative

## 5. REEL STRUCTURE RECOMMENDATIONS

### Cut Timing & Pacing

| Content Type | Cut Length | Total Duration |
|--------------|------------|----------------|
| **Hook** | 0:00-0:03 (3s) | Critical - stop the scroll |
| **Race Action** | 0:03-0:15 (12s) | 2-3s cuts, build energy |
| **Data/Reveal** | 0:15-0:25 (10s) | Slower, let numbers land |
| **Emotional Beat** | 0:25-0:35 (10s) | Horse close-ups, stills |
| **Proof/Authority** | 0:35-0:45 (10s) | Institutional shots |
| **CTA** | 0:45-0:55 (10s) | Clear, direct, gold accent |
| **Outro** | 0:55-0:60 (5s) | Logo lockup, tagline |

### Transitions
**Recommended:**
- Hard cuts (90% of transitions)
- Gold flash wipe (high-energy moments)
- Match cut (horse motion to data)
- J-cut (audio leads video)

**Avoid:**
- Star wipes, page turns (amateur)
- Slow fades (kills energy)
- Complex multi-step transitions (distracting)

### Hook Patterns That Work

**For Racing Content:**
1. **The Contrarian:** "First rule of ownership... you're using the wrong numbers"
2. **The Result First:** "$1.5M. Ellerslie. 2-year-old colt."
3. **The Question:** "What does FMA-regulated racehorse ownership look like?"
4. **The Visual Shock:** Dramatic finish line cross + gold flash

### Text Overlay Best Practices
- **Font:** Helvetica Bold or Futura (per editing guide)
- **Color:** Gold (#D4AF37) for emphasis, white for body
- **Position:** Lower third (classic) or centered (impact)
- **Animation:** Reveal from bottom, 0.5s ease-out
- **Max Length:** 6-8 words per overlay

### What Evolution Stables Is Getting Right

2. ✅ Clear differentiation from traditional syndicates
3. ✅ FMA compliance as core proof point
