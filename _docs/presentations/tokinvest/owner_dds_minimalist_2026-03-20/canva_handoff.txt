Slide 5
Goal: Lead into the owner case.
Layout: Three compact content blocks.
Kicker: For horse owners
Headline: Your ownership stake, working harder.
Block 1 title: Liquidity
Block 1 body: Create a new capital pathway around an existing asset. Fractional stakes can be listed on a regulated marketplace, allowing owners to broaden participation without changing the underlying care, management, or campaign structure.
Block 2 title: Control
Block 2 body: Owners decide whether to list, how much participation to offer, and how the opportunity is structured. Owners remain central to the horse, the campaign, and the decision-making around both.
Block 3 title: Equity
Block 3 body: Owners retain the underlying equity position in the horse. The model is designed to sit around that equity, not replace it, with value anchored in a real horse, a real campaign, and a real ownership structure.
