## Overview

The appeal here is obvious: turning research notes into narrated animated
videos could be a fast way to explain dense material to non-technical
stakeholders. For horse-racing research, investor updates, or internal brief
playback, that kind of format could reduce the friction between analysis and
communication.

This still looks more like a premium demo feature than a core workflow for the
current stack. The reported value is presentation speed, but the tradeoff is
subscription gating, limited control, and overlap with other NotebookLM-shaped
ideas already parked on the radar.
