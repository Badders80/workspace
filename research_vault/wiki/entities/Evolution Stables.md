The canonical workspace for Evolution Stables operations. Located at `/home/evo/workspace`. Governed by DNA (Decisions, Norms, Architecture) and supported by a research vault for knowledge capture.

`/home/evo/workspace` is the active build surface. `/home/evo/` is control plane only (dotfiles, wrappers, auth, global tool config).
