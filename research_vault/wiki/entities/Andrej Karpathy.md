# Andrej Karpathy

Computer scientist, AI researcher, and educator. Former Director of AI at Tesla. Known for educational content on neural networks, LLMs, and AI engineering. Proposed the LLM Wiki pattern for personal knowledge management.

## Key Contributions

- **LLM Wiki pattern** — a structured approach to building persistent, compounding knowledge bases with LLMs [^source: raw/karpathy-llm-wiki.md#L1-L75]
- **Educational content** — widely followed tutorials and courses on deep learning and LLMs
- **Tesla Autopilot** — led the computer vision team for autonomous driving
