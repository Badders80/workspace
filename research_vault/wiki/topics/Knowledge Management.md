# Knowledge Management

The practice of capturing, organizing, and retrieving information to support decision-making and learning. The Karpathy LLM Wiki pattern is one approach to AI-assisted knowledge management.

## Approaches

- **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** — index raw documents, retrieve chunks at query time. No accumulation between sessions.
- **LLM Wiki** — LLM incrementally builds a persistent wiki. Knowledge compounds with every source.
- **Zettelkasten** — atomic notes with stable IDs. Immutable, linked, human-curated.

## Key Principles

- Raw sources stay immutable
- Synthesis is persistent, not ephemeral
- Cross-references are maintained automatically
- Contradictions are surfaced, not resolved silently
