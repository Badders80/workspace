# Capture Inbox

This is the intake surface for fast notes and imported research.

## Use This For

- quick founder notes such as "Met X, discussed Y"
- article or social captures that still need normalization
- imported crawl batches that need review

## Fast Paths

- Manual note template: [[_templates/Manual Capture]]
- Normalized note template: [[_templates/Normalized Note]]
- Command-line capture: `just research-capture "Met X at Karaka" "Talked about tokenized ownership and investor appetite."`

## Capture Rule

Fast capture first. Clean structure second.

The note only needs enough information to preserve the signal:
- who
- what
- why it mattered
- what to follow up
